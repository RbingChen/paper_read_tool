该文本是 OneRec 技术报告的核心部分，主要涵盖三个方面的内容：首先，通过在线 A/B 测试数据（表 12）展示了 OneRec 推荐系统在快手平台（KuaishouApp 和 Kuaishou LiteApp）上的性能改进，比较了基础 OneRec 和带 RM 选择的 OneRec 在多个用户指标（如停留时间、观看时间等）上的绝对提升百分比。结果显示，带 RM 选择的版本在大多数指标上显著优于当前多阶段系统。其次，文本简要描述了技术优化，包括使用交叉注意力（cross-attention）、专家混合（MoE）等技术，结合批处理（batching）和 MPS 技术，实现了 5 倍吞吐量提升和 28.8% 的 MFU（模型浮点运算利用率）。最后，结论部分详细介绍了 OneRec 的架构：它是一个端到端的生成推荐系统，基于编码器-解码器（encoder-decoder）模型，利用编码器压缩用户终身行为序列以提取兴趣，并通过 MoE 扩展解码器参数；后训练阶段采用定制强化学习（RL）框架优化推荐；工程优化大幅提升了训练和推理效率（MFU 达 23.7% 和 28.6%），同时降低了运营成本（OPEX 仅为传统系统的 10.6%）。文本还指出局限性，如推理阶段扩展问题，并强调 OneRec 在效果和效率上的优势。

5. Conclusion, Limitations, and Future Directions In this paper, we introduce OneRec, a novel end-to-end generative recommendation architecture. Built as an encoder-decoder model, it compresses users’ lifelong behavior sequences via its encoder to derive user interests, while leveraging Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) to massively scale decoder parameters for precise short-video recommendation decoding. During post-training, we develop a customized reinforcement learning (RL) framework to refine recommendations by aligning model outputs with the reward function. Thanks to meticulous engineering optimizations, OneRec achieves 23.7% and 28.6% Model FLOPs Utilization (MFU) in training and inference — a dramatic improvement from single-digit baselines—closing the gap with the mainstream AI community. Notably, this compute-intensive design operates at 10.6% the OPEX of conventional recommender systems. Comprehensive evaluations demonstrate that OneRec has surpassed existing recommendation systems in both effectiveness and efficiency.
